How to get UPI SMS
1
Open Messages app on your phone
2
Search for "debited" or "UPI" or your bank name
3
Select multiple SMS messages and copy
4
Paste them all at once in the box on the left
5
Review the parsed transactions, then Import

Onyx parses UPI debit/credit alerts from all major banks and payment apps entirely on-device. Your SMS messages are never sent to any server — only the extracted transaction data is saved.
